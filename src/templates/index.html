Hotel
Mercury Hotel

The Mercure Kyiv Congress Hotel is located just a short ride away from Kiev's city centre and is part of the Cosmopolite Shopping and Entertainment Centre. The hotel is within easy access from all relevant business and leisure facilities. Prospect Peremohy is the direct road connection to the city centre. Shuliavska metro stop is just a few minutes' walk away. Offering a unique design of avant-garde style dedicated to famous artists, Mercure Kyiv Congress is located just few minute's walk from Shuliavska Metro Station. Stylish rooms offer free Wi-Fi and tea/coffee facilities. Guests are offered free access to the hotel's fitness centre and outdoor pool.

A flat-screen TV, hairdryer and a safety deposit box are provided in all the air-conditioned rooms at the Mercure Kyiv Congress. Dark wooden furniture and colourful paintings give each one a contemporary look.

Delicious meals of French and Bavarian cuisine are served in the Brasserie restaurant and Mercure Kyiv Congress private brewery with high ceilings and panoramic windows is working on site. There is also an equipped congress hall at the property.

Kiev Railway Station is 5 km away, Kiev Airport is 10 km away. Boryspil International Airport - 39 km away. Hotel adjoins Cosmopolite Entertainment Centre, a full range of conference service is also available.

Solomjanskyj is a great choice for travellers interested in city walks, churches and sightseeing.

This property is also rated for the best value in Kiev! Guests are getting more for their money when compared to other properties in this city. The staff speak English
